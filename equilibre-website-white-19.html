Our Office
Based in Prague, one of Europe's most beautiful cities
We chose Prague as our home — a city where centuries of history meet a thriving tech scene, world-class talent, and an unbeatable quality of life.

A city built for deep work. Prague consistently ranks among the top cities in Europe for quality of life, safety, and affordability. Its compact size, excellent public transport, and vibrant café culture make it the perfect place for a team that values focus and collaboration.

World-class technical talent. Home to Charles University and the Czech Technical University, Prague produces exceptional mathematicians, computer scientists, and engineers — many of whom have gone on to lead research at DeepMind, Google, and top quantitative firms.

At the heart of Europe. Two hours from Berlin, Vienna, and Munich by air, Prague sits at the crossroads of European finance and technology. Our team works closely with partners across the continent while enjoying one of the world's most walkable and inspiring cities.

A growing tech hub. The Czech Republic has become one of Central Europe's leading startup ecosystems, with a deep bench of AI and engineering talent, favorable business conditions, and a culture that rewards technical excellence.
